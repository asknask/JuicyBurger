Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Juicy Burger LLC ("Juicy Burger," "we," "us," or "our") operates the juicyburger.com website and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our phone ordering system, or interact with our SMS messaging services.

By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including but not limited to:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and delivery address
  • Order Information: Food orders, preferences, dietary restrictions, and payment information
  • Communication Data: Records of phone calls with our automated ordering system and SMS message history

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our website, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website or source

3. SMS Messaging and Consent

3.1 Double Opt-In Process

Juicy Burger uses a double opt-in consent process for all SMS communications. Here's how it works:

  1. Initial Contact: When you call our phone ordering line (661-374-0660), our AI voice assistant will help you with your request.
  2. Consent Request: If you request menu links, order confirmations, invoices, or other information to be sent via SMS, our system will first send you a consent message asking for your explicit permission.
  3. Explicit Consent: You must reply "YES" to the consent message before we send any additional SMS messages.
  4. Ongoing Communication: Only after you confirm consent will you receive the requested information and any related order updates.

3.2 Types of SMS Messages

With your consent, you may receive the following types of messages:

  • Menu links and ordering information
  • Order confirmations and status updates
  • Invoices and receipts
  • Delivery notifications
  • Responses to your customer service inquiries

3.3 Message Frequency and Costs

Message frequency varies based on your interactions and requests. Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply. You are responsible for any fees charged by your carrier.

3.4 Opting Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message from us. For help with our SMS service, reply HELP to any message.

3.5 Mobile Information Sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To process, fulfill, and deliver your food orders
  • Customer Service: To respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Communications: To send you order updates, receipts, and requested information (with your consent for SMS)
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our offerings
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who assist in operating our business (payment processors, delivery services, SMS platform providers)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Juicy Burger, our customers, or others

We require all third-party service providers to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring
  • Employee training on data protection practices

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Order records and SMS consent data are retained in accordance with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal exceptions)
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a new "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
